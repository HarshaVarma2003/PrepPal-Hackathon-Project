About PrepPal

Welcome to GITAM PrepPal,
GITAM PrepPal is your ultimate companion for exam preparation at GITAM. We offer past question papers, model tests, and important questions, all designed to streamline your study process.

Our Mission


Our mission is to empower GITAM students with the resources they need to excel in their exams. We aim to provide a comprehensive, user-friendly, and engaging study platform.

Key Features


Past Question Papers: Easily find and review previous years' exam papers. Model Tests: Practice with our model tests to gauge your preparedness. Important Questions: Focus on the questions that matter the most. Personalized Study Plans: Get study plans tailored to your schedule and progress. Progress Tracking: Monitor your improvement and stay motivated.
